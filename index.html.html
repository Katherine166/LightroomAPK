A Guide to Creating and Sharing Lightroom Presets on iOS

Adobe Lightroom for iOS offers powerful editing capabilities right at your fingertips, allowing you to enhance your photos on the go. One of the standout features of Lightrooms is the ability to create and share presets, which are custom editing settings that can be applied to your photos with a single tap. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of creating and sharing Lightroom presets on iOS, so you can streamline your editing workflow and share your unique style with others.

1. Creating a Preset:

  • Open Lightroom on your iOS device and select a photo you'd like to edit.
  • Tap the Edit icon (the sliders) to access the editing tools.
  • Make adjustments to your photo using the various editing tools available, such as exposure, contrast, color, and more.
  • Once you're satisfied with your edits, tap the Presets icon (three overlapping circles) at the bottom of the screen.
  • Tap the "+" icon to create a new preset.
  • Give your preset a name and optionally, assign it to a group.
  • Select which editing settings you want to include in your preset by toggling the corresponding sliders.
  • Tap the checkmark icon to save your preset.

2. Sharing a Preset:

  • To share a preset with others, tap the Presets icon (three overlapping circles) at the bottom of the screen.
  • Long-press on the preset you want to share until a menu appears.
  • Tap "Share" from the menu options.
  • Choose how you want to share the preset, such as via AirDrop, Messages, Email, or other compatible apps.
  • Follow the prompts to send the preset to the recipient.

3. Importing a Shared Preset:

  • If you receive a shared preset from someone else, you can easily import it into Lightroom on your iOS device.
  • Open the message or email containing the shared preset and tap on the attached preset file.
  • Lightroom will automatically open and prompt you to import the preset.
  • Tap "Import" to add the preset to your Lightroom presets library.

4. Applying a Preset:

  • To apply a preset to a photo, open the photo you want to edit in Lightroom.
  • Tap the Edit icon (the sliders) to access the editing tools.
  • Scroll down to the Presets section and tap on the preset you want to apply.
  • Lightroom will automatically apply the preset to your photo, and you can further adjust the settings if needed.

Conclusion: Creating and sharing Lightroom presets on iOS is a convenient way to streamline your editing workflow and share your editing style with others. Whether you're creating presets for personal use or sharing them with friends, colleagues, or followers, Lightroom makes it easy to customize your photos and achieve stunning results with just a tap. So go ahead, unleash your creativity, and start sharing your unique presets with the world!
